# Kingfisher

Meet Kingfisher - a bird known for precision, patience, and striking only when the setup is right. That is exactly what the current version of this bot is built to do.

Kingfisher is now a positional trading bot. Instead of chasing ultra-fast scalping moves and closing trades within seconds, it is designed to build and manage positions over a longer window. In practice, that means trades can stay open for minutes to hours while the bot manages entries, pending orders, spacing, profit-taking, and cycle-level risk rules.

The older scalper generation laid the foundation for this system, but the current live model is different. Today, Kingfisher focuses on structured position management rather than pure speed.
